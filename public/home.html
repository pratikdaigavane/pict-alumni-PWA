  • fiber_manual_recordPICT ACM Student chapter (PASC)keyboard_arrow_down
    • PICT ACM Student chapter (PASC) is one of the fastest growing student communities in PICT.

      Having been setup in 2011, in the last 8 years PASC now has over 500 active members.

      The chapter has been taking significant strides, by organising various special interest groups and technical sessions throughout the year to guide the students.

      The annual fest of the chapter Pulzion has become one of the flagship events of the college.

      Event is supported by industries like Capgemini, Tieto, Reliance industries and well absorbed by students of Pune and outside Pune.

      This time pulzion has observed 15 technical and non-technical events, 5000+ footfall and industry partners like CapGemini.

      The contributions of the chapter have not gone unnoticed and PASC has won The Best Student Chapter in India from ACM India for the last two consecutive years.

      PASC is eagerly waiting for collaboration with PICT alumni for guidance.
  • fiber_manual_recordEntrepreneurship Development Cell and Business Incubation Center keyboard_arrow_down
    • The PICT Entrepreneurship Development Cell (E-Cell) aims towards guiding the students and providing them with a platform for developing their entrepreneurial skills. It is a cell run and driven by the management of the institute. It not only motivates the students but also provides them with the necessary resources to become entrepreneurs.
    • E-Cell conducts various activities such as workshops, interactions with entrepreneurs, case studies, building prototypes, etc. to cultivate various skill sets like organizing, team building, sales and marketing, relationship building, getting exposure to the real life marketing challenges and analysing the new marketing trends etc. which is a really important part of start-ups.
    • The Business Incubation Center (BIC) is a platform where we provide not only the guidance for the business startups but also provide various services and facilities like electricity, hardware requirements etc. Till now 18 startups have been incubated in this Incubation Centre. The one which is being incubated right now is the Gauss and Reimann Scientific.
    • Cell has organized various events till date like Entrepreneur’s Meet 2016, INNOVATIA, Entrepreneur’s Meet 2018 (MAGNATE).
      Entrepreneur’s Meet for this year is scheduled on 29th February 2020.
